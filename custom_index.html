About Us

“Light is more important than the lantern” - Nizar Qabbani

Technology today isn’t reserved for the blue chips or the tech nerds of silicon valley. Running a successful small business in this digital age depends on a good mix of technolgies. R Six Eight is here to find the right mix to help your small business achieve its goals.

Our foundation is built ontop two main components: design first and great user experiences. No matter which of our services your business works with--Web Development, IT Services, Tech Consulting--you will receive a level of attention, honesty, and focus as if we were a partner in your business.

R Six Eight’s foundation and name originates from Jason Killelea’s (CEO) career in stage lighting where he was drawn to the color Parry Sky Blue (code R68) which was named after his mentor Tony Award winning lighting designer, Chris Parry. Jason’s business background comes from co-founding Visual Lighting Technologies (VLT) where he served as Vice President. After growing VLT for 10 years, Jason decided to take his business experience and design sensibilities to start R Six Eight.
